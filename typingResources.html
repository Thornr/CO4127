Getting Started With Touch Typing

Nanotale is not meant to be a touch typing tutor, while it does help players improve their speed, it doesn't teach correct form. If you'd like to up your typing game, check out some of the hand picked free resources below.

KeyBr

As the adage goes, practice makes perfect or perhaps more accurately, practice makes permanent. Keybr is a training tool that encourages typists to use proper form, it's a great tool for people that can't keep their eyes off the keyboard or are pecking away at their keys.

Typelit

Typlelit is a typing practice tool that allows users to improve their touch typing speed and accuracy by retyping entire classic books. If you create an account you can keep track of your typing speed and accuracy. You can brag to all three of your friends that you have not only read Ulysses, you've typed it too!

TypeRacer

TypeRacer is a typing practice tool that allows users to compete in races against each other, users are able to keep track of their statistics
